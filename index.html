TT/OK Application Tool

Your comprehensive guide to managing Telegraphic Transfers, tracking payments, and securing vehicle reservations with precision.

Core Principles

The TT/OK Tool is the backbone of our reservation system. Follow these Golden Rules to ensure accuracy and speed.

1 TT = 1 App

Never create multiple applications for the same transfer slip.

Accuracy First

Data entered here determines Income Allocation. Double check everything.

One to Many

Allocate one large payment to multiple cars using "Add more Stocks".

Submission Workflow

Follow this sequence when you receive a new TT Copy (deposit or full payment).

1. Verification

Ensure the TT Copy image is clear and meets the minimum deposit requirement.

2. Data Entry

Navigate to New TT Application. Enter Currency, Amount, Payer Name, and Date exactly as they appear on the slip.

3. Fund Allocation

Select the Stock No. and amount.
Multi-car payment? Click + Add more Stocks to split the funds.

4. Attachments

Upload the TT Slip or Online Banking Report.

STOP: Do not upload SWIFT copies here. SWIFT copies are strictly "Supporting Documents" only.

5. Finalize

Add staff notes (e.g., "Payment 1 of 3") and click ADD.

Handling Balances

Scenario B: Leftover Balance

FAQ

Can I upload a SWIFT copy as my main TT Copy?
No. SWIFT copies are only for the "Supporting Documents" section. For the main TT/OK hold, you must use the remittance slip or the detailed online banking report.
Can I submit one TT Copy for multiple cars?
Yes. Use the "+ Add more Stocks" button within the single application. Do not create separate applications for the same slip.
What if the transfer amount is less than the deposit?
The application will be registered, but the unit will remain in pending status. It will not switch to "TT/OK YES" until the minimum requirement is met via a top-up.
